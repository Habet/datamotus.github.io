Skills demand estimate based on web scraping and data mining tools

The goal

To use online job advertisements to assess the skills demand in Armenian job market. The research also had to cover the dynamics of change in the demand for specific skills, industry, and professions. The tool that will be developed, will help to follow and estimate vacancy rate by industry in real-time. The auxiliary goal of the study was to give HR managers and HR portals information on how the typical job post is composed, what are the most frequent components, required experience for different types of jobs, etc.

What we did

We conducted research using web scraping, text mining and predictive analysis tools and Association rules mining techniques. The summary of results can be found on SlideShare.

  • Client: American University of Armenia (research grant)
