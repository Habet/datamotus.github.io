Customer churn modeling – feasibility study

The goal

To conduct a feasibility study to assess the plausibility of adopting churn modeling practice within the company.

What we did

We analyzed of the company’s CRM data to assess its applicability for churn modeling. The client was provided with recommendations on the data collection, storage, and management for the churn modeling process.

  • Client: Telecom, Armenia
