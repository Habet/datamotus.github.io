Predictive model for an HR portal

The goal

To create a predictive model for an HR portal to predict the number of online job applications submitted to the given job post over time. This would help the client to make efficient pricing decisions.

What we did

The project involved extensive data cleaning and data preparation, merging different datasets and data transformation and time series forecasting to reach the desired goal.
